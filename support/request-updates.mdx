## Domain logos

For incorrect or missing logos when looking up by domain, submit an update request:

<Card title="Submit a logo update" icon="arrow-up-right-from-square" href="https://www.logo.dev/update">
  Report wrong logos or request new brands to be added
</Card>

## Ticker, ISIN, and crypto logos

For issues with stock ticker mappings, ISIN codes, or cryptocurrency symbols, email us directly:

<Card title="Email support" icon="envelope" href="mailto:support@logo.dev?subject=Ticker%20mapping%20issue">
  Report ticker symbol or ISIN mapping problems
</Card>

Include the following in your email:
- The ticker symbol, ISIN, or crypto symbol
- The exchange (if applicable)
- The expected company or asset
- A link to verify the correct mapping (optional but helpful)
